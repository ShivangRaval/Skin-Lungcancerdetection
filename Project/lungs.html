Note : Our Lung Cancer Prediction Project is committed to aiding individuals in the early detection and classification of lung cancer using H&E (Hematoxylin and Eosin) staining images. This powerful technique provides critical insights into lung tissue structures, enabling accurate cancer assessment. It serves as a valuable resource for those who suspect lung health issues. However, it's essential to understand that uploading H&E staining images of healthy lung tissue may lead to inconclusive results, as our system relies on recognizing cancer-related visual indicators. We encourage users to utilize our service when genuine concerns or symptoms related to lung health arise.
